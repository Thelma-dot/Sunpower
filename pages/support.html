Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common support questions

How do I monitor my solar system performance?

You can monitor your system through our mobile app or web portal. The app provides real-time data on energy production, consumption, and savings. You'll also receive monthly performance reports via email.

What should I do if my system stops producing energy?

First, check if there's a power outage or if the inverter display shows any error codes. Check the circuit breakers and ensure the system is properly connected. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team immediately.

How often should I clean my solar panels?

We recommend cleaning your panels every 3-6 months, or more frequently in dusty areas. Use a soft brush, mild detergent, and water. Avoid abrasive materials that could scratch the panels.

What is covered under warranty?

Our warranty covers 5-10 years for solar panels, 2 years for inverters, and 1 years for workmanship. This includes defects in materials, performance guarantees, and installation quality issues.

Can I add more panels to my existing system?

Yes, in most cases you can expand your system. However, this depends on your roof space, electrical capacity, and local regulations. Contact us for a free assessment of expansion possibilities.

What happens during a power outage?

Without battery storage, your solar system will automatically shut off during outages for safety reasons. With battery storage, you can continue to power essential appliances during outages.
