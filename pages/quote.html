Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our solar solutions

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations take 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of the system. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer comprehensive warranties including 25-year panel warranty, 10-year inverter warranty, and 5-year workmanship warranty on all installations.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer various financing options including solar loans, leases, and power purchase agreements to make solar accessible for everyone.

How much can I save on my energy bills?

Most customers see 70-90% reduction in their energy bills. The exact savings depend on your current usage, roof size, and local electricity rates.
