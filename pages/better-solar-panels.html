Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our solar panels

Do panels work on cloudy days?

Yes. Output is lower than in full sun, but quality panels still generate power in diffuse light.

How long do panels last?

Panels routinely exceed 25 years with proper installation and maintenance.

Will they damage my roof?

No. We use code-compliant mounting with proper flashing to protect your roof.

Can I expand later?

In most cases yes. We can assess roof space and electrical capacity for add-ons.
