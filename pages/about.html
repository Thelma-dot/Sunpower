Our Story

Founded in 2019, SunPower Electrical Engineering began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make clean, renewable energy accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a leading provider of solar solutions across the region.

Our journey began when our founders recognized the urgent need for sustainable energy solutions in our community. With over 20 years of combined experience in electrical engineering and renewable energy, they set out to create a company that would not only provide top-quality solar products but also deliver exceptional service and support.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 500 families and businesses transition to clean energy, reducing their carbon footprint while saving thousands of dollars on energy costs. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction continues to drive us forward as we work toward a more sustainable future.

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy by providing innovative, reliable, and affordable solar solutions that empower individuals and businesses to take control of their energy future.

Our Vision

To create a world where clean, renewable energy is the primary source of power, making our planet healthier and more sustainable for future generations.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction guide everything we do. We believe in doing business the right way while making a positive impact on our community and environment.
